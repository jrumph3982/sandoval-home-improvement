Who We Are

More Than a Decade
of Trusted Service

Sandoval Home Improvement was founded with a simple belief: every homeowner deserves reliable, high-quality work at a fair price. Since our start, we've served hundreds of families across the Northern Virginia, DC, and Maryland area — earning a reputation built on honesty, craftsmanship, and genuine care.

As a locally owned business, we treat every customer like a neighbor — because many of them are. We live in the same communities we serve, and that accountability drives everything we do.

From small handyman jobs to full home renovations, our team brings the same commitment to every project regardless of size.
